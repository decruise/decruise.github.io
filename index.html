The world's most advanced longboard.






Welcome to our capstone project!


We are going to deliver major improvements on the biggest pain points with existing longboards, and build the safest, easiest to ride board in the world.


Suspension
Automotive-grade suspension system to give the most comfortable ride of any skateboard.
Rider Assist
Computer vision system to warn for approaching vehicles in the rider's blind spot.
Electric Drivetrain
Remote-controlled electric motors to provide easy acceleration and braking.
Custom Battery
Custom Lithium-Ion battery pack to power the system and maximize range.
Lighting & Horn
Controllable lights and horn to improve safety and bystander awareness.
Weatherproofing
Water-resistant components and headlights, enabling riding in the rain and the dark.

Feel free to check out the About page or progress logs for more information.
